    What is LetsSnap?

    LetsSnap is a non-profit organization founded by Tanushree Koshti, Drishya Nair, and Hrishitha Mulakkayala. These three girls have founded LetsSnap to spark a love for coding in young children and to promote diversity in the field of computer science. LetsSnap hosts coding classes for elementary and middle school children and uses the money raised from class fees to help fund computer science scholarships for deserving people who are unable to pursue computer science for reasons beyond their control.
